About Certificate Verification

All Hexafy certificates contain a unique identification number that can be verified through this system. This helps employers and institutions confirm the authenticity of your credentials.

Where to find your certificate number?

Your certificate number is located at the bottom right corner of your issued certificate. It follows the format HEX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX.

Certificate not found?

If your certificate cannot be verified, please contact our support team with a copy of your certificate for assistance.
